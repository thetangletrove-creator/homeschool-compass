# Prompt A: Homeschool Compass Competitive Landscape Research

## Objective
Map the competitive landscape for ESA/homeschool compliance tools. We need feature-by-feature comparison, pricing, app store presence, and review sentiment to identify gaps and position Homeschool Compass.

> ⚠️ **CRITICAL: Live-verify all App Store/Play Store ratings.** Do not source from third-party review aggregation sites or blog posts. Open the actual App Store page and read the listed rating + rating count directly. If an app has no public store listing (e.g. enterprise-only like ClassWallet), say so explicitly. Rating inflation from review roundups is a known problem — a 4.2 claimed from an aggregator may be 3.3 on the actual store.

## Deliverable Structure

### Part A: Competitive Matrix (table format)

| Feature | Homeschool Compass | Competitor 1 | Competitor 2 | ... |
|---------|-------------------|--------------|--------------|-----|

Rows:
- Monthly price ($)
- Annual price ($)
- Free tier available?
- ESA bank integration?
- Bill tracking?
- Compliance calendar?
- Receipt/document management?
- State-specific forms?
- Curriculum planning?
- Mobile app (iOS/Android)?
- Offline mode?
- Multi-child support?
- Payment processing (Stripe)?
- Public records integration?
- Auto-reminders/notifications?

### Part B: Pricing Ladder

For each competitor:
- Entry price point
- Mid-tier
- Premium
- What each tier unlocks
- Free trial length (if any)
- Discounts (annual vs monthly, multi-child, military, etc.)

### Part C: Gap Analysis

1. What features exist in the market that we don't offer yet? (tag: MISSING)
2. What features we have that no competitor offers? (tag: MOAT)
3. What features competitors offer poorly that we could do better? (tag: OPPORTUNITY)
4. Most common complaints in negative reviews across ALL competitors — 5-10 specific pain points

### Part D: App Store Intelligence

> ⚠️ **Live-verify platform claims.** A product that was "web-responsive only" 18 months ago may now have native iOS/Android apps. Check the App Store and Play Store publication dates. A recent launch (within 6-12 months) means mobile is a strategic priority for them, not a differentiator for us.

For each app with iOS/Android presence:
- Current rating
- Rating volume (number of ratings)
- Last update date
- Notable negative review themes (3-5 summaries)
- Notable positive review themes (3-5 summaries)

### Part E: Strategic Summary

1. **Top 3 threats** — which competitors most directly threaten our position
2. **Top 3 opportunities** — underserved needs we can capture
3. **Top 3 pricing insights** — pricing psychology lessons from the market
4. **Recommendation** — where should Homeschool Compass differentiate?
