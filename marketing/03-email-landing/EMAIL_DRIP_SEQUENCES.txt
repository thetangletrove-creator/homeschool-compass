--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Resend (resend.com): Free to 3,000 contacts/month. Good for MVP.
2. Loops (loops.so): Free to 1,000 contacts. Better automation UI.
3. Mailchimp: Free to 500 contacts. More mature but overkill for now.
4. Tag every subscriber with: signup_date, state, source (landing page, scorecard, etc.), plan (free/paid)
5. Use dynamic fields: [State], [First Name], [X]/100 score, [Grade]
6. Set up webhook from Stripe → email platform to trigger paid sequences
