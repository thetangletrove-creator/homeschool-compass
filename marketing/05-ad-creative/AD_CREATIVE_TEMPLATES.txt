A/B TEST PLAN (Agent runs these, tracks results)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Test 1: Headline
Variant A: "[State] Homeschool Requirements 2026"
Variant B: "Is [State] Homeschool-Friendly? (Grade: [A-F])"

Test 2: Body Copy

Variant B: Score-focused (BODY B)
Metric: Cost per click (CPC)

Test 3: Image
Variant A: State grade card
Variant B: Checklist preview
Metric: CTR + landing page scroll depth

Test 4: CTA Button
Variant A: "Learn More"
Variant B: "Sign Up"
Metric: Landing page conversion rate
Duration: 7 days
Budget: $20/day per variant
Winner: Higher conversion

Test 5: Audience
Variant A: Interest targeting (HSLDA, homeschool curriculum)
Variant B: Lookalike 1% of website visitors
Metric: Cost per lead (CPL)
Duration: 14 days
Budget: $30/day per variant
Winner: Lower CPL

AD ACCOUNT WARMING PROTOCOL (Weeks 1-4)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Week 1-2: Engagement Campaigns
Objective: Engagement
Budget: $10-20/day
Audience: Broad interests (homeschooling, parenting, education)
Creative: Helpful tips, "Did you know?" posts, scorecard graphics
Goal: Likes, comments, shares. Build social proof.

Week 3: Traffic Campaigns
Objective: Traffic
Budget: $20-30/day
Audience: Engagers from Week 1-2 + 1% lookalike
Creative: "See how your state ranks" → links to scorecard
Goal: Website visits, pixel fires

Week 4: Lead Generation
Objective: Leads
Budget: $30-50/day
Audience: Website visitors + lookalikes
Creative: "Free [State] Homeschool Compliance Checklist"
Form: Name, email, state, compliance worry
Goal: Email list growth

Week 5+: Conversion Campaigns
Objective: Conversions
Budget: $50-100/day
Audience: Warm (email list, visitors, video viewers) + lookalikes
Creative: Testimonial-style, urgency, value
Goal: Paid conversions

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
□ Total ad spend

□ CTR (click-through rate)
