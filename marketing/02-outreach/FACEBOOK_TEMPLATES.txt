RULES FOR EVERY ANSWER:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Read the group rules before posting. Screenshot them.
2. Never spam. One answer per thread. No copy-paste across multiple threads.
3. Never argue. If someone disagrees, say "Thanks for sharing your experience!" and move on.
4. Never give legal advice. Always say "Here's what the statute says" or "The [State] DOE requires..."
5. Never post your link unless (a) asked, or (b) group rules allow resource sharing.
6. Use your real first name. Not the product name. Not a fake name.
7. Be helpful first. The product mention is a bonus, not the goal.
