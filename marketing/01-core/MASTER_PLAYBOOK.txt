DECISION TREE — When to STOP and email boss
================================================================================

[Ad account restricted] → STOP all campaigns → Screenshot error → Email boss
[Post removed by group admin] → STOP posting in that group → Note in log → Find new group
[Someone asks for legal advice] → Reply "I'm not an attorney, but here's what the statute says..." → Include disclaimer
[Website down or 500 errors] → Check Vercel dashboard → Email boss if down > 10 min
[Negative review/comment] → Reply politely → Screenshot → Email boss
[Email open rate < 20% for 2 weeks] → Try re-engagement sequence (in EMAIL_DRIP.txt) → Note in report
[Ad CTR < 1% for 3 days] → Pause creative → Try new creative next week
[Someone asks about pricing in a group] → Reply with link to pricing page → Do NOT negotiate in comments
